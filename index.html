About the Project

Crown of Shadows is a solo-developed open-world action RPG set in a semi-historical feudal era where political fracture, hidden conspiracies and supernatural mystic forces collide. Players step into the role of a highly skilled ninja who moves through bustling villages, dense forests, mountain temples and shadow-soaked valleys to uncover a conspiracy that threatens to plunge the kingdom into darkness.

Current Development Focus

  • Combat abilities and animation systems
  • Camera and movement polish
  • Enemy behavior and encounters
